About me

I am a software engineer with experience in JavaScript, Python, React, as well as both relational and NoSQL databases. My background in technical support largely informs my philosophy with regard to programming, focusing on accessable, user friendly designs. I am also a fan of test driven development.

Software improves our lives and I am thrilled to be part of an industry that can make such an impact on the world. I am fueled by my passion for learning and discovery, eager to build on my knowledge and expand my skillset. Currently a freelancer and looking for new opportunities so let's chat.
